Quora

This web page is delivering many options to try to match what the visitor is interested in reading. Challenges include supplying many unrelated bits of information succictly, conveying the many ways in which the viewer can interact with the site's content, and providing an attractive page suitable for browsing and skimming.



aeon magazine

This web site points out the organization's most important recent content, as well as allowing a visitor to navigate to topics of interest. Challenges include balancing availability of content with clarity and focus so that the visitor does not feel overwhelmed with choices.

nytimes

This web site aims to show the vast amount of new content provided by the NYTimes on a daily basis. The challenges are to provide a front-page experience (with images, headlines to skim) while also allowing for intuitive navigation to topics of interest or interaction with web-embedded applications.
